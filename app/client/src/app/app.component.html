Ferrari

The horse was originally the symbol of Count Francesco Baracca, a legendary "asso" (ace) of the Italian air force during World War I, who painted it on the side of his planes.

Ferrari

Spyker

The company's motto is "For the tenacious, no road is impassable". The marque's logo displays the rotary engine of an airplane, a reference to the historic Spyker company which manufactured aircraft.

Spyker

Lamborghini

The Lamborghini logo shows a bull or Taurus that is the brand founder's zodiac sign. Ferruccio Lamborghini was extremely interested in Spanish bullfighting sport that is also shown in the logo.

Lamborghini

Aston Martin

The Aston Martin name came into existence in 1914 following one of Lionel Martin's successful runs at the Aston Hill Climb in Buckinghamshire, England.

Aston Martin

Porsche

Porsche's company logo was based on the coat of arms of the Free People's State of Württemberg of former Weimar Germany, which had Stuttgart as its capital.

Porsche
